Get neighbors of a vertex p.

get neighbors of a vertex p pxp, pxm are x-neighbors to the right (plus) and left (minus) and so on for y and z dimensions

Parameters
pis the input point
pxpis the x-neighbor to the right (plus)
pxmis the x-neighbor to the left (minus)
pypis the y-neighbor to the right (plus)
pymis the y-neighbor to the left (minus)
one_over_dx_is the reciprocal of the dx corresponding to this neighbor
